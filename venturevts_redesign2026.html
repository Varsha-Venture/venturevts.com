About VTS

Who We Are

Ventest Solutions (VTS) is an affiliated company to Venture Instrumentation Technologies Pvt. Ltd. — staffed by the most knowledgeable and experienced professionals in material characterisation.

Our state-of-the-art facility houses instruments from only the top global brands in the market, trusted by scientists worldwide. We promise that the unparalleled quality of our skills and services will deliver real value to your processes, products and brands.

Through product validation, failure analysis, material benchmarking, regulatory compliance, and R&D support, we enable businesses to build confidence in their engineering decisions with fast, accurate, and cost-efficient material insights.

Mission

To deliver precise, reliable, and affordable testing services by leveraging our expertise to exceed customer expectations.

Vision

To set the benchmark for innovation and reliability in advanced testing services globally.

Purpose

To simplify testing services by combining speed, reliability, and affordability under one roof.
